About Muddy Boots Services

Muddy Boots Services was founded with the intention of ensuring all clients have all of their needs met. Serving areas in the Mideast/Northeast Missouri areas, we cover locations from the St. Louis Area, Union, Washington, Foristell, Troy, Bowling Green, Hannibal, and Palmyra, we want to make sure all of our customers are completely satisfied.  We strive to help all of our customers achieve their goals, whether that be new landscaping, excavation, land development, lawn services, something done in our woodshop, or general upgrades. We will work with you to make sure all of your needs are either met or exceeded, and we will be sure to keep you updated every step of the way.
